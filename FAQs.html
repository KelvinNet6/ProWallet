Frequently Asked Questions


What is PaySheet?

PaySheet is a platform that helps you manage and track your payments, transfers, and account settings in one place.


How can I sign out of my account?

You can sign out by clicking the "Sign Out" button in the sidebar or by going to your account settings page.


How do I transfer funds?

You can transfer funds by clicking on the "Transfer Funds" section from the sidebar, where you can enter the amount and recipient details.


How can I change my account settings?

To change your account settings, visit the "Account Settings" page from the sidebar, where you can update your information and preferences.
