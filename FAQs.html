Frequently Asked Questions

What is PaySheet?

PaySheet is a secure digital wallet platform that allows you to manage your money, make transfers, and perform contactless payments all in one place. It's designed to make your financial transactions simple, fast, and secure.

What are the transaction limits?

Our current transaction limits are:
• Daily Transfer: Up to MWK 10,000,000
• Weekly Withdrawal: Up to MWK 25,000,000
• Monthly Transaction: Up to MWK 50,000,000

How do I transfer funds?

To transfer funds:
1. Click on "TF" in the sidebar menu
2. Enter the recipient's PaySheet number
3. Enter the amount you wish to transfer
4. Verify the transaction details
5. Enter your security code to confirm

How does contactless payment work?

Our contactless payment feature allows you to pay at supported terminals by:
1. Opening the Contactless Pay section
2. Enabling contactless payment
3. Holding your device near the payment terminal
4. Waiting for confirmation

How secure is PaySheet?

PaySheet uses advanced security measures including:
• Two-factor authentication
• End-to-end encryption
• Secure PIN verification
• Real-time transaction monitoring

How can I contact support?

• Phone: 1-800-PAYSHEET
• In-app chat support
